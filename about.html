About Ananya Events

Creating magical moments and joyful memories since 2014

Our Story

Founded in 2014 with a simple mission: to make every celebration memorable and joyful. Ananya Events began as a small family business in Visakhapatnam with a passion for creating beautiful, meaningful experiences.

Over the years, we've grown from organizing small family gatherings to managing large-scale corporate events, weddings, and celebrations. Our journey has been marked by countless smiles, happy tears, and the joy of bringing people together.

Today, we're proud to be Visakhapatnam's premier event management company, known for our creativity, professionalism, and the personal touch we bring to every event.
